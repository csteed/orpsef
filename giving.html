ORPSEF Ways to Give

There are various ways to make gifts to ORPSEF. Your contribution can be made as a one time gift or can be pledged over a period of years. Some of the most common forms of donations to the ORPSEF are:

Cash / Checks
These are the easiest gifts to value for tax purposes and are deductible in the year in which they are made.

Credit/Debit Cards
Credit/Debit card gifts are convenient for the donor and like cash gifts, are deductible in the year that the gift is made. You can make a secure gift online with this method using the trusted PayPal service (click the Donate button).

Securities
Gifts such as stock or bonds may be donated to ORPSEF with a minimal out-of-pocket cost. The amount of the deduction is based on current value and the method of transferring securities differs depending on how they are currently held.

Corporate Matching Gifts
A method of making an even greater impact is through corporate matching gifts. Many companies will match employee contributions to educational institutions and could possibly double or triple your gift. Please contact us to find out if your company offers a matching gift program.

Make a Gift Today!
If you are interested in learning more on how you can make a gift today please contact us. You may also utilize our contribution form or if you wish to make a secure online gift now using PayPal please click the donate button below.

Giving Made Easy!

Another easy way to contribute is through the Amazon Smile program. Amazon will donate a 0.5% of the price of eligible AmazonSmile purchase to ORPSEF if you utilize our registered link below.
